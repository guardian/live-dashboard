The Guardian’s SEO Dashboard

What is the SEO Dashboard?

The SEO dashboard is an experimental live view of what's happening on the Guardian website right now.

Is it a replacement for Omniture?

No. The reporting provided here is made on a best efforts basis, but wouldn't be suitable for an ABCe audit. There are many jobs for which Omniture remains the best tool.

You said it's experimental, does that mean it might go away?

Yes, but that's less likely to happen if you let us know it's useful to you.

Who's it for?

It's available to all Guardian staff. It's been developed with ideas from Chris Moran and Mary Hamilton providing feedback, but we hope it can help everyone make decisions.
